üìä Karst Risk Indicators for Underwriters

  • Bedrock Type: Limestone, dolomite, gypsum = high dissolution potential
  • Groundwater Depth: Shallow water tables accelerate karst development
  • Development Pressure: Construction dewatering and vibration trigger collapses
  • Historical Density: Past sinkholes in area indicate active terrain
  • Seasonal Patterns: Wet seasons increase collapse frequency (spring/summer)
  • Urban Infrastructure: Leaking water/sewer mains dissolve subsurface voids
