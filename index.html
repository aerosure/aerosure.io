FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to help you make informed decisions

What happens if weather prevents flying?

We monitor conditions closely and reschedule at no charge. For storm damage documentation, we prioritize clear weather windows to capture evidence quickly.

Do I need to be present during inspection?

Not required for exterior inspections. We just need property access confirmation. For construction sites, coordinate with your site manager.

What's included in the standard report?

PDF report with findings summary, 25+ annotated high-resolution images, issue identification with severity ratings, and recommendations. Video clips available on request.

How quickly will I receive my report?

Standard delivery is 24 hours. Storm damage documentation can be expedited to same-day for insurance claims. Raw images available immediately if needed.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. FAA Part 107 certified pilot with $1M liability insurance. Certificate of insurance available upon request for commercial clients.

What areas do you service?

Richmond, Henrico, Chesterfield, and surrounding counties within 50 miles. Travel fees may apply beyond 30 miles.

What about privacy and neighboring properties?

We focus exclusively on your property and follow strict privacy protocols. Neighboring properties are not photographed without permission.

How do you price larger or complex projects?

Base pricing covers standard residential and small commercial. Larger properties, multiple buildings, or specialized requirements are quoted based on scope. Volume discounts available.
