FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to help you make informed decisions

What happens if weather prevents flying?

We monitor conditions closely and reschedule at no charge. For storm damage documentation, we prioritize clear weather windows to capture evidence quickly. Our Flight Ops center provides real-time weather tracking for transparency.

Do I need to be present during inspection?

Not required for exterior inspections. We just need property access confirmation. For interior or detailed inspections, we'll coordinate with you directly.

How quickly can you respond to storm damage?

Our StormCheck™ service offers 2-hour emergency response during daylight hours. We actively monitor tropical systems and pre-position for rapid deployment when storms threaten Virginia.

What deliverables do I receive?

All packages include high-resolution images (4K), detailed inspection report, and secure cloud access. Insurance packages include carrier-specific formatting and direct submission capability.

Are you insured for commercial operations?

Yes, we carry $1M liability insurance specifically for commercial drone operations. Certificate of insurance available upon request. All flights comply with FAA Part 107 regulations.

How do you price larger projects?

Base pricing covers standard residential and small commercial properties. Larger properties, multiple buildings, or specialized requirements are quoted based on scope. Volume discounts available for regular monitoring.
