Severity Classification

Four-Tier Priority System

Every defect categorized by structural impact and repair urgency.

Critical

Immediate structural risk. Active leaks, major structural damage, safety hazards requiring emergency repair.

High

Significant damage likely to worsen. Missing shingles, failed flashing, compromised penetrations.

Moderate

Repair recommended within 6-12 months. Cracking, blistering, early-stage deterioration.

Low

Monitor during next inspection. Minor wear, cosmetic issues, normal aging patterns.
